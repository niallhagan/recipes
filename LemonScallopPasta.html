 1) Cook the pasta. 2) Heat the oil and butter in a pan, add the shallots, garlic, thyme, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper to the pan and cook for a few minutes.

3) Add the wine and chicken stock and cook for 5 minutes.

4) Add the basil, lemon juice and zest and cook for another few minutes.

5) Add the fish and a little more butter then stir and add the pasta when the fish is cooked.
