Chimichurri Sauce

A South American sauce that goes with steak, chicken, lamb, pork and fish.

Ingredients

Method
1 tightly packed cup (40g) parsley leaves finely minced

1 tightly packed tbsp fresh coriander

2 tsp finely minced garlic

1 tsp dried chilli flakes

60ml red wine vinegar

125ml extra-virgin olive oil

salt and freshly ground black pepper

 1) Blend everything together.
