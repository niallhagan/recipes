Jerk Chicken Thighs

An easy jerk chicken thatâ€™s on the table in less than an hour. Serve with rice.

Ingredients

Method
2 cloves of garlic roughly chopped

2cm/1 inch piece of fresh root ginger roughly chopped

1 yellow scotch bonnet chilli, chopped or 1 tsp dried chilli flakes

1 tbsp chopped fresh thyme leaves

1 tsp coriander seeds or 1/2 tsp ground coriander

2 cloves or a pinch of ground cloves

1/2 tsp ground mixed spice

1 tsp curry powder

salt and freshly ground pepper

2 tbsp vegetable oil

2 boneless chicken thighs

25g/1oz butter

lemon wedge to serve

 1) Take all the ingredients and blend together. If too thick add a small amount of water.

2) Marinade the chicken thighs.

3) Cook.

4) Serve with rice and coriander.
