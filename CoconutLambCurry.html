Coconut Lamb Curry

Ingredients

Method
2 tbsp sunflower oil

1 onion thinly sliced

2 tsp grated fresh root ginger

2 tsp crushed ground garlic

1 tsp crushed cinnamon

20 curry leaves

2 tbsp mild curry powder

1 tbsp ground coriander

1 tsp ground tumeric

1 tsp chilli powder

625g boneless lamb in chunks

400ml coconut milk

200ml vegetable stock

100g fresh coconut

6 tbsp chopped fresh coriander

salt and pepper

 1) Heat the oil in a large pan. Add the onion and stir-fry over a medium heat for 4-5 minutes.

2) Stir in the ginger, garlic, cinnamon, curry leaves, curry powder, ground coriander, tumeric and chilli powder.

3) Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes and then add the lamb.

4) Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes and then stir in the coconut milk and stock. Bring to the boil, season well and cover tightly.

5) Cook over a very low heat, stirring occasionally for 2 hours or until the lamb is tender.

6) Remove from the heat and splinkle over the grated coconut and chopped fresh coriander before serving.
