Mushroom Gravy

Ingredients

Method
30g butter

1 chopped shallot

250g sliced mushrooms

300ml beef stock

1 tbsp chopped parsley

1 tsp chopped fresh thyme

salt and freshly ground black pepper

 1) Melt the butter in a pan.

2) Add the shallot and cook for 2 minutes until soft.

3) Add the mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes.

4) Pour in the stock and simmer for about 5 minutes.

5) Add the parsley, thyme then season to taste.
