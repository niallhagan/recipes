Lemongrass Chicken

Vietnamese Lemongrass Chicken.

Ingredients

Method
1 lb chicken breast or thighs. Boneless, skinless and cubed

3 or 4 stalks of lemongrass (white tender part only)

2 tsp cornstarch

1 tsp sugar

1 tsp pepper

2 tbsp fish sauce

4 tbsp peanut, vegetable or sesame oil

2 tbsp shallots or scallions, minced

2 cloves garlic

1 chopped red bell pepper

1‚ÅÑ4 cup water

1 tbsp brown sugar or caramelized sugar syrup

 1) In a large bowl, mix the cubed chicken with the lemongrass, cornstarch, sugar, pepper, 1 tbsp of the fish sauce and 1 tbsp of the oil. Set aside to marinade for at least 1 hour or up to 8 hours.

2) Heat the remaining 3 tbsps of oil in a wok or large pot over a medium-high flame. Add the scallions or shallots and stir fry for about 30 seconds.

3) Add the chopped bell pepper and continue to stir fry until thoroughly heated and beginning to wilt, 3 or 4 minutes.

4) Add the chicken and its marinade and continue to stir fry until the chicken is just cooked through, another 3 or 4 minutes.

5) Reduce heat to medium-low and stir in the water, remaining fish sauce and brown sugar or caramel syrup. Simmer for another 1 - 2 minutes. Adjust seasoning to taste and serve with steamed rice.
