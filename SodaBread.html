Soda Bread

Ingredients

Method
1lb plain flour

1/2 pint buttermilk

1/2 tsp bicarb soda

1/2 tsp salt

 1) Sieve all dry ingredients together and make a well in the centre and add milk to make a thick dough.

2) Place mixture on a floured board and knead.

3) Flatten the dough into a circle.

4) Place on baking sheet and divide into 4.

5) Bake in oven for 40 mins at 205 C.
