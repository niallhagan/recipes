Wheaten Bread

Ingredients

Method
2 cups wheatmeal

1 cup plain flour

1 tsp bicarb soda

3⁄4 tsp salt

1 oz margarine

3⁄4 pint of buttermilk

 1) Mix all the ingredients together and work in the margerine.

2) Use the buttermilk to make a medium dough and knead.

3) Turn into greased lined tin.

5) Bake in oven for 1 hour at 200 C.
